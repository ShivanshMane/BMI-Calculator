BMI Calculator

How to use the BMI Calculator:

1. Enter your height in inches in the "Height" field.

2. Enter your weight in kilograms in the "Weight" field.

3. Click the "Calculate BMI" button to get your BMI result.

4. The result will be displayed below, along with a classification (Underweight, Normal weight, Overweight, or Obese).
